Apple Chips Recipe

Description

Apple chips are a delectable snack made from thinly sliced apples that are typically baked until they achieve a crisp and crunchy texture. These chips offer a delightful blend of natural sweetness and a satisfying crunch, making them a healthier alternative to traditional potato chips. Often seasoned with various spices or left unadorned to highlight the fruit's inherent flavor, apple chips provide a convenient way to enjoy the essence of apples in a portable and crispy form.

Ingredients

  • 2 Golden Delicious apples, cored and thinly sliced
  • 1.5 teaspoons white sugar
  • 0.5 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 225 degrees F (110 degrees C).
  2. Arrange apple slices in a single layer on a metal baking sheet.
  3. Mix together sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl; sprinkle over apple slices.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until apples are dried and edges curl, 45 minutes to 1 hour. Use a metal spatula to transfer chips to a wire rack until cool and crispy.

Tips

Slice apples using the slicing blade of a food processor or mandoline. If desired, you can flip the apple slices halfway through baking and sprinkle them with more cinnamon sugar.
